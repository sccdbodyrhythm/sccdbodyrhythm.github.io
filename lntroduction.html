Body Rhythm Q&A

本系於1997年由謝大立教授成立，人體舞動則為媒傳系大一新生必經的課程，我們在為期八週的時間裡，配合燈光、音效，並以結構裝置作為媒介，最後透過肢體將抽象的情感呈現在人體裝置上。

本課程除了曾受邀到北美館、北藝大、台大等地公演外，更榮獲2007年德國紅點最佳品質設計教育獎，備受國際肯定。

2020年恰逢肺炎疫情擴散，原訂的人體舞動公開展演也因應防疫措施改為線上呈現，將現實的舞台移至網路世界中。

本學年人體舞動展演由謝大立教授、曲家瑞老師、陳威志老師、劉邦耀老師、牛俊強老師、陳俐縈老師、顏瑞儀老師指導，與全體大一學生共同創作。

 

策展組:彭語安、李沁、莊立霆、李宛儒、邱宇堂

洗手舞: 許惠斐、殷語彤、王亮晰、陳貞友

路徑⇌位移

「換了媒介，但我們傳達的本質不變。」

路徑是起始點到達目的地所經歷的總長；而位移是起始點連接目的地的直線距離。2020年肺炎疫情籠罩全球，人與人傳遞與接收訊息的距離被迫拉長，交流轉成影像；互動切換網頁；舞台移至銀幕。肉眼從即時接收轉化成隔著一層13、5寸的媒介，但人體的溫度和舞動的力量不變。

The Department of Communications Design was founded by professor 謝大立, and "Body Rhythm" is an required course for all freshmen. We express our abstract emotions through structural installation, lights, sounds and most importantly, our bodies. Body Rhythm was invited to perform at places such as Taipei Fine Arts Museum, Taipei National University of the Arts and National Taiwan University, and has won the Red Dot Best Quality Design Education Award in 2007.

 

In 2020, the Body Rhythm public performance was turned into online exhibition due to the COVID-19 epidemic. The stage in the reality was thus moved into the cyberspace.

 

The exhibition "Body Rhythm" this year is guided by professor 謝大立, T.曲家瑞, T.陳威志, T.劉邦耀, T.牛俊強, T.陳俐縈 and T.顏瑞儀, and is co-created by all freshmen.

Concept

"The medium changed, but the messege of our communication remains the same."

Distance is the total length one thing travels from the starting point to its destination, while displacement is a vector whose length is the shortest distance between the initial and the final position. 2020, the COVID-19 epidemic hit. The distance between communicating people was lengthened, face to face conversations were turned into online images, interactions were changed into online forms and performance stages were relocated on to screens. Although the receiving process is now complicated by a 13.5 inch screen, the original warmth of human body and the power of our dancing rhythm remains the same.
